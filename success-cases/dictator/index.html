Optimizing Efficiency: Dictator Streamlines Operations

Dictator

Our

Best practices

DICTATOR is a group of companies that was born in 1932 on a worldwide scale, manufacturers of industrial components and elevators with a special presence in Europe. This success story dates back to 2016 when we were asked for a digital marketing strategy for the Spanish market with the aim of increasing its relevance online and thus increasing both its organic positioning and the volume of qualitative leads. For this, we developed an SEO positioning strategy, a new website accessible to all devices with a careful architecture, designed for a powerful organic positioning combined with a content strategy that contrasted with those of the competition due to its high qualitative value. Thanks to a web maintenance and SEO strategy based on good practice and continuous improvement over time, we have achieved results that far exceed client expectations.
