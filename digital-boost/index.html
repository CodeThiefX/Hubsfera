SEO

Position your website organically in Google for the most relevant keywords of your business and increase traffic exponentially.

We have been working on SEO for a decade in many sectors, we have learned which are the best practices for rankings and we have a consolidated methodology, without shortcuts, based on good practice, perseverance and data analysis.

We use the most avant-garde data extraction and traceability systems to adjust each detail that helps us improve the relevance of your project on a digital level and thus overcome the fiercest competitors.

1. SEO Audit

Data is the key to success. In order for SEO to have a real impact on your business, we carry out an extensive analysis of both, the positioning of your brand on the internet and that of your competitors. This report helps us understand the current situation, where we can improve and where we need to go. At the same time, it provides us with key information on how competitors are positioning themselves and what tools they use to do so.

2. Positioning strategy and keywords selection

Based on the information extracted from the SEO audit and your short, medium and long-term objectives, we develop a strategy where we define the most relevant actions to take to improve the project’s rating and select the keywords to position.

3. SEO On and Off Page implementation

Constancy and good practice combined with data driven strategy based on tangible objectives are our recipe for natural SEO positioning, without the risk of penalties and in constant improvement. Our methodology is focused on qualitative traffic safely, without tricks or experiments.

PPC / SEM

Attract qualitative traffic to your website and increase your sales immediately.

We impact your audience through ads in an accurate way, we work with the objective of achieving the best possible performance thanks to the analysis of all the elements that make up the campaign, from the ad, the audience or keywords and the landing page, creating a qualified funnel in continuous optimization.

1. Paid strategy definition

We set the settings, audience and keyword parameters based on our data analysis. We use the most advanced analytics tools in the industry to carry out a precise and consistent analysis to create the best strategy for each product and service.

We create the composition of campaigns and ad groups with the aim of offering each search or user the most accurate content possible, thus achieving a high campaign rating and more effective performance.

2. Paid campaign configuration

We create unique accurate ads for each type of campaign, taking into account all the analysis carried out and the communication of each brand. The combination of these 2 factors results in a better click-per-impression ratio, while always maintaining the brand’s personality.

Our campaigns work continuously to adjust and improve their performance, so the campaigns are always in continuous optimization. In this way we achieve a progressive and continuous improvement with higher performance.
